🛡️ Stay Safe Online Checklist

✔ General Safety Tips:

● Use strong and unique passwords for all accounts
● Enable two-factor authentication (2FA)
● Do not click on unknown or suspicious links
● Verify emails/SMS before responding
● Keep software and apps updated
● Avoid downloading files from untrusted sources
● Log out from shared/public devices
● Use secure Wi-Fi networks

💰 UPI / Financial Safety Tips:

● Never share OTP, PIN, or UPI credentials
● Always verify UPI ID before sending money
● Avoid scanning unknown QR codes
● Double-check recipient details before payment
● Use official banking/payment apps only
